Asia Cup

The asia cup is the biennial cricket tournament organised by the ASIA CRICKET COUNCIL. The competition is contested by Asian members. It was established in 1983 whwn the ASIAN CRICKET COUNCIL was founded as a measure to ptomote goodwill between Asian Countries, making it the only continential championship in cricket in which the winning team becomes the champion of Asia.

INDIA vs PAKISTAN

The India-Pakistan cricket rivalry is one of the most intense sport rivalries in the wotld.Showdowns between the teams are considered some of the biggest matches in the world, and are among the most-viewed sport games. The tense relations between the two two nations, resulting from the bitter diplomatic relations and conflict that orginated during the Partition of British India into India and Pakistan in 1947, the Indo-Pakistan wars, and the Kashmir conflict, laid the foundations for the emergence of a fierce sporting rivalry between the two nations who had shared a common cricket heritage.
