ELEMENTS OF WEATHER

METEROLOGISTS (scientists who study weather) measure several elements to describe it accurately. ~TEMPERATURE:How hot and cold the air is. It is measured by thermometer. ~HUMIDITY:The amount of water vapour in the air. High humidity makea the air feel sticky. ~AIR PRESSURE:The weight of the air pressing on Earth's surface, measured by a barometer. ~WIND:The movement of air from high pressure areas to low pressure ones.

WEATHER FORECASTING

Today, scientists use satellites, radar and computers to predict weather.They collect data about temperature, humidity, and wind speed from different places and analyze it. Weather forcasts help people prepare for storms, floods, or heatwaves and save lives. Even a simple phone weather app gives useful daily updates.
