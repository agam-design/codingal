INDRODUCTION

WEATHER is the condition in the air or atmosphere at a particular time and place.It describes how the sky looks and feels whether it is sunny, cloudy, windy or rainy. Unlike climate which remains almost the same for years,weather changes dailyor even hourly. For example it may be bright and warm in the morning and turn cloudy and cool by evening. These short-term changes make weather one of the most fascinating feature of nature

WHAT CAUSES WEATHER?

The main cause of weather isSun.The Sun's heat warms different parts of earth unevenly because the Earth is round.This uneven heating makes air move, creating winds.Warm air rises and cool air comes down, forming clouds, rain and storms. The Earth's rotation and tilt also influence weather patterns, leading to changing temperatures, winds and rainfall in different regions.
