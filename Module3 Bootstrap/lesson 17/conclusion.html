DIFFERENT TYPES OF WEATHERS

1.Sunny Weather:The sky is clear, and the sun a shines brightly. It is warm and cheerful, perfect for outdoor activities. 2.Cloudy Weather:Clouds cover the sky and block sunlight, making the day cooler. 3.Rainy Weather:When clouds become heay with water vapor, it rains. Rain refreshes the Earth and helps plant grow. 4.Windy Weather: Strong winds blow, sometimes causing dust storms or waves in sea. 5.Stormy Weather:Thunder lightining or heavy rain occur. Storms can be dangerous but are natural parts of weather cycle. 6.Snowny Weather:In cold regions water freezes into snowflakes and falls to the ground creating a white blanket.
